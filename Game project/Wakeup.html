Wakeup: Medina Thrills

Intern AI Game Programmer at Herodot Studio

Description

"WakeUp" is an asymmetrical mobile survival horror game. That mixes horror, strategy, and teamwork. The Survivors look for the Shrines around the map and capture the orb. and for The killer, he should sabotage the Shrines before the survivors place it on the safe zone.

Contribution

My partner and I collaborated on our end-of-year project in AI Development to enhance the game's immersion. We implemented a behavior tree system, enabling players to enjoy the game with bots, even without a large player base.
